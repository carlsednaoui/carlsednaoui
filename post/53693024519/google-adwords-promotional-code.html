Google Adwords Promotional Code

Entering a Google Adwords promotional code after you’ve entered your payment details is relatively easy, it’s just that the menu is hard to find — hence this blog post.

Once you’ve entered your payment information in Adwords go to “Billing” > “Billing Preferences”:

Then click “More actions” > “Apply a promotional code”:

Et voila! You’re done.
