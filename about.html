Permutationer

About: Sinatra app built to help create Adwords campaigns.

When it’s time to launch your campaigns on Google Adwords, nothing is more painful than having to create 100’s of keyword permutations. Well, fear no more, [Permutationer](http://permutationer.com/) is here to help you create keyword permutations in seconds.

Fun fact: We used this tool at Profero to relaunch the Western Union account, which had over 1 million keywords.
